What Charon does

Charon watches Pump.fun tokens in real time. When a token triggers a signal, Charon gathers data, runs it through filters, asks an AI to pick the best one, then paper-trades or buys live depending on your mode.

1

WATCH — Signal ingestion

Listens for fee claims on-chain (WebSocket), polls graduated coins every 30s, polls trending tokens every 60s. Token must appear in at least 2 sources to proceed.

2

GATHER — Enrichment

Fetches price, mcap, liquidity, holders, chart context, saved wallet cross-reference, and Twitter narrative from 5 sources in parallel.

3

FILTER — Hard gates

Runs every enriched token through the strategy's filter rules. Fail any one rule → rejected immediately. No exceptions.

4

AI PICK — LLM batch screen

Up to 10 passing candidates are sent to Mimo v2.5-pro in one batch. The AI picks at most 1 BUY. Must meet minimum confidence threshold.

5

TRADE — Execution

In dry_run: simulates the trade in SQLite. In confirm: sends a Telegram button. In live: submits a Jupiter swap immediately.

After a position opens, Charon monitors it every 10 seconds and auto-closes on TP, SL, or trailing stop.

Stage 1 — Signal Sources

Charon has 3 "eyes". For the Sniper strategy, a token must appear in at least 2 eyes before it's considered a candidate.

👁 Eye 1 — Fee Claims (real-time WebSocket)

Connected to Solana blockchain. Detects when a Pump.fun token's creator distributes fee revenue. A large fee claim = real trading happened.

Pre-filter: fee < 2 SOL → dropped instantly. Fee ≥ 2 SOL → checks other eyes.

👁 Eye 2 — Graduated coins (every 30s)

Polls Pump.fun API for coins that crossed the bonding curve and launched on Raydium. Keeps a rolling 2-hour window in memory. Stale entries are pruned each cycle.

👁 Eye 3 — Trending tokens (every 60s)

Polls Jupiter top-trending: top 100 tokens by volume in the last 5 minutes. Only .pump addresses kept. Filtered by rug ratio and bundler rate. Stays in memory for 10 minutes.

Most tokens are killed here. Without fee claim + graduated/trending overlap, Charon doesn't even start enrichment.

Stage 4 — AI Batch Screen

Tokens passing all filters go into a queue (max 10, max age 10 min). Every new passing candidate triggers a full batch call to the AI.

Token name, mint, Twitter handle
Market cap, price, liquidity, holder count
Fee claim amount and recipient breakdown
Trending rank, volume, swaps, rug ratio, bundler rate
Chart: distance from ATH, top-blast risk
Which of your 750 wallets hold this token
Twitter narrative (if available)
Up to 6 active lessons from /learn
Which filters passed/failed

verdict: BUY, WATCH, or PASS

Stage 5 — Execution

After AI approval, Charon re-fetches fresh data one final time before executing. If the token still passes, it acts based on your mode.

ModeWhat happensYour status
dry_runSimulated trade stored in SQLite. No real money. 0.1 SOL paper size.ACTIVE
confirmSends Telegram Approve/Reject buttons. You decide before anything executes.inactive
liveImmediately signs and submits Jupiter swap. No approval step.inactive
Slippage simulation (CHARON-02): In dry_run, entry mcap is recorded 1% above spot to simulate buy slippage. Exit applies 1% slippage and 0.2% fee deduction. This makes dry-run P&L more realistic.

What dry run does NOT simulate

Price impact of your own buy
Solana gas fees (~0.000005 SOL per tx)
Real fill price variation

Position Monitoring

Charon checks every open position every 10 seconds against Jupiter price data.

Exit rules (Sniper defaults)

RuleThresholdStatusHow it works
Take Profit (TP)+50%ONCloses if mcap is 50% above entry
Stop Loss (SL)-25%ONCloses if mcap is 25% below entry
Trailing TP20% pullbackONArms at first TP hit. Closes if price drops 20% from peak.
Max Hold—OFFWould force-close after N minutes if set
Partial TP—OFFWould sell a % at a target and hold the rest

P&L formula

pnl_percent = (current_mcap / entry_mcap − 1) × 100
pnl_sol      = position_size_sol × pnl_percent / 100

On auto-exit (with slippage sim):
effective_exit  = exit_mcap × 0.99      ← 1% slippage
fee_deduction   = 0.1 SOL × 0.002     ← 0.2% fee
final_pnl_sol   = simulated_pnl − fee_deduction
